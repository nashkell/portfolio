Cosa website redesign
—
My mandate was to re-design COSA’s current website into one that communicates a confident professional feel, a clean aesthetic, and elegent simplicity. The client informed me that the website was visited by people from all over the globe with all different types of internet connections. An overall challenge was making the site functionality and design capable to be developed in basic HTML/CSS while keeping within the comfort zone of the company’s developer; as well as create a more fluid experience for the user. In the process I revised the navigation, FAQ, Team, and partnerships sections to allow a more comfortable pacing to the amount of imformation provided.

Design is based off the “960.grid” with a fixed navigation bar to allow for ease of use. COSA plans to launch the new design in the fall 2013.
