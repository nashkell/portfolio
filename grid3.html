Cold cases stay cold
—
I was given the task of choosing an article from the front page of the New York Times, finding the system of the article, and determining a method to aggregate the data. I chose to create an interactive method that a user could access via the article on the New York Times website Link. The article covers incidents that occured during the civil rights era and how the families are reacting to the FBI’s cold case initiative. The end user would be able to learn about the cold case initiative and then the subsequent cases. When viewing any specific case, details about the case, families reactions, and samples letters from the FBI would be provided.

Concept designed in a 16:9 format, with responsive capability for mobile platforms.
