Old and new architecture
—
A study of new an old architecture in the city of Philadelphia, PA. I trekked through philadelphia and shot images of the many different types of architecture, from gothic to more modern buildings. The goal was to capture an essence of the different types of architecture that make Philadelphia so unique. What is presented are two different compositions combining these images, one based on a grid and the other organic.
