epa.gov Poster Series
—
This poster series grew from a project of understanding visual relationships and how to use them to create visual impact and clarity while solving communications problems. Focusing on the idea of the word when and the implications of “when is enough enough?", and "when is to much to much?”, I proceeded to look at this idea from an environmental perspective. Finally, after studying a multitude of research found through the EPA, I produced this poster series as an attempt at creating awareness of these environmental plights.

Printed on Ilford Galerie Smooth Matte Canvas, Inkjet 20”x30”.
