J. Shoemaker Promotional Material
—
The client saught a few spreads, ranging from simple to ornate, to advertise the glass work he perfected while studying at the Corning Museum of glass. All glass pieces were collected from the client and photographed in studio. These are meant to be presented in a booklet, yet to be made, to allow him the ability show any prospective clients what he was capable of.

Below is one of two promotional videos for the client. The goal was to create a video from images of his work and the hot glass shop, that showcased John’s work. This also provided a perspective on the intimate gritty setting where the beautiful pieces, shown later in the video, are created. I also recorded John speaking about his passion of working with glass, which was dubbed over a choice of music that fit the mood.
