Typographic Journey
—
I was charged with creating a 30-second to one-minute typographic animation; utilizing typographic expression, sound, color, and type treatments to visualize the meaning of the text through typography and motion. Through this process, I developed a concept—sketch, storyboard, plan, and created a short motion graphic work that can be viewed below.

The final format is for web broadcast: 960 X 540.

Script
—
After leaving the comfort of my humble home I set out towards the nearest subway entrance. A nauseous stench of bodily fluids mixes with the exhaust of the passing cars and train system below. As I approach the platform I hear the sounds of other potential passengers rushing through turnstiles. I reach into my pocket to pick my fare. Now other passengers about to embark surround me, anxiously awaiting until the oncoming subway car stops. And not until the jittering doors slide open that my adventure begins.
