About
—
Graphic designer based in NY building effective and meaningful experiences through user-centered design and dedicated research making connections informationally, thematically, and aesthetically. Enhancing avenues of communication through a focus on typography, web design, UX/UI design, interactive design, and motion graphics.

My body of work currently includes pieces ranging from website design to motion graphics and print. Located in TriBeCa, New York; I am a graphic designer currently working at Poulin+Morriswhilst exploring work with an emphasis on typography, information, wayfinding, interface design, motion graphics, and other forms of visual and audial communication and problem solving.

I am confident in Adobe InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects. Experienced in print and digital design. Proficient with HTML, CSS, jQuery and Javascript.
